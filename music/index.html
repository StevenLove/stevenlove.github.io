Synthesizer

Alternate contexts for notes

Here you can type in scale degrees of notes in a progression to get a list of other ways to interpret those notes, along with a score of each interpretation's dissonance.
This could help you see that a 2-5-7 is actually probably a 5-1-3!
Valid notes are as follows: 1, b2, 2, b3, 3, 4, b5, 5, b6, 6, b7, 7. Just type them with spaces between them



Synthesizer Controls

Notes are played by pressing keys in your home row: namely, CapsLock, a, s, d, f, g, h, j, k, l, ;, ', and Enter
The root of your scale can be modified by raising it with b or by lowering it with v
The mode of your scale can be similarly modified with m and n
